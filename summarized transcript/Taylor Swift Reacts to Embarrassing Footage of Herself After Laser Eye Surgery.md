# Taylor Swift Reacts to Embarrassing Footage of Herself After Laser Eye Surgery

## Summary
Taylor Swift appears on Jimmy Fallon's show shortly after getting Lasik eye surgery. Jimmy reveals he secretly contacted Taylor's mom, who filmed Taylor while she was loopy on post-surgery painkillers. The video shows Taylor confused over a banana that "doesn't have a head," tearfully insisting it isn't the one she wanted, while wearing protective goggles taped to her face. Taylor is shocked and amused that Jimmy went behind her back to her mom for the footage.

## Key Moments
- Taylor confirms she had Lasik and was given strong post-surgery pills.
- Surprise video premiere of Taylor crying over a headless banana while her mom tries to comfort her.
- Taylor jokes about her low alcohol tolerance, referencing the "#DrunkTaylor" trending moment after just 2.5 mojitos at a party — she goes "from zero to thinking I'm a wizard within two drinks."
- Coins the hashtag "#LasikLover" on the spot.
- Teases her upcoming SNL appearance: she will perform "Lover" in a new way and debut a song she has never performed live before.

## Tone
Light, comedic, embarrassing-on-purpose talk-show segment.
