# Priyanka Chopra Jonas Talks Returning to Indian Cinema and Playing a Pirate in The Bluff

## Summary
Priyanka Chopra Jonas appears on a late-night show to promote her new pirate film "The Bluff" (#1 in 28 countries) and announce her return to Indian cinema after 6–7 years with S.S. Rajamouli's epic "Varanasi" (releasing April 2027), shot in IMAX over a 20-month schedule.

## Highlights
- **"The Bluff"** is set in the 19th century Caribbean (Cayman Islands), centered on overlooked female pirates of history (Mary Read, Grace O'Malley, Ching Shih). Directed by Frank Flowers, produced by the Russo brothers and Angela Russo. She drew inspiration from Uma Thurman's The Bride in *Kill Bill* — a mother who will kill to protect her child.
- **Behind the scenes**: Her then-2.5-year-old daughter visited set when Priyanka was head-to-toe in fake blood eating a hamburger; Priyanka took her to the makeup trailer to show her it was pretend — a sweet bonding moment.
- **Bluffing game**: Jimmy reveals all three of his statements were true (in Baseball Hall of Fame for *Fever Pitch*, Harrison Ford pierced his ear, pitched a song to Paul McCartney). Priyanka tricks him on all three of hers — she does NOT have a pilot's license, she HAS eaten worms (Mopane worms, a Zimbabwean delicacy), and she CAN move each eyeball independently (demonstrates live).
- **"Varanasi"**: Described as epic, IMAX-format, global release.

## Tone
Promotional but warm and playful late-night interview.
