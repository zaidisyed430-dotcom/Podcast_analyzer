# Islam's Secret World: What the Quran Reveals About Jinns & Aliens | Ft. Imran Hussein | The Ranveer Show

## Summary
Ranveer Allahbadia (TRS) interviews Imran Hussein, a UK-based Islamic scholar from Sapiens Institute, on jinn, the occult, and the modern UFO phenomenon. The conversation aims to bridge classical Islamic theology with contemporary paranormal/UAP discourse.

## Foundations: What Are Jinn?
- Etymology: Arabic root meaning "hidden" — beings of the unseen realm.
- Created by God *before* humans, made from "smokeless fire" (Imran speculates this could be plasma; humans were made from clay).
- Have free will like humans — therefore both good and evil jinn exist.
- "Shaytan" in the Qur'an applies to evil humans too, not only jinn.

## Origin Story of Iblis (Shaytan/Satan)
- Originally a devout jinn who rose to the rank of the angels through worship.
- When God created Adam and ordered all to bow in respect, Iblis refused — argued "I am made of fire, he is of clay." Was cursed and given time until Judgment Day to mislead humans.
- Iblis's main weapon is **waswasa** (whispering) — disguised as your own internal voice, building gradually.

## Capabilities of Jinn (per Quran/Hadith)
- Three main classes: those that fly, those that take form of dogs/snakes, and those that come and go.
- Can shape-shift into humans, animals, sometimes inanimate objects/plants.
- Mentioned as "builders and divers" in the story of Solomon — built things, dived oceans, even teleported the Queen of Sheba's throne.
- Can affect humans through dreams.
- Pre-Islam, jinn could ascend to the heavens to extract future knowledge and feed it to oracles — banned after the advent of the Prophet ﷺ.

## Why Jinn Approach Humans (and Vice Versa)
- Good jinn generally don't interact with humans — only the evil ones do, usually via:
  - Magicians/mediums (Arabic *sihr*, Hindi *jadu*)
  - Falling in love with / becoming attached to a human
  - Retaliation if a jinn is unintentionally harmed (e.g., disturbed in their dwelling place)
- Cross-cultural pattern: every culture (Irish faeries, Native American skin-walkers, Greek demons) describes the same kind of in-between beings.
- "One of Satan's greatest tricks is convincing people he doesn't exist."

## Cost of Engaging Jinn — Aleister Crowley & Jack Parsons
- Crowley (most famous 20th-c. occultist) summoned an entity called **Lam** — the painted depiction is "almost identical" to the modern gray alien.
- Crowley required disciples (including rocket pioneer Jack Parsons) to perform "despicable" sex rituals — jinn never help for free, and what they ask is always more than what's asked of them.
- The Greeks called the same entities "demons" and tried to contact them.

## The UFO / Jinn Connection (the modern angle)
- 2017 NYT article and Lou Elizondo's whistleblowing brought UFOs/UAPs into mainstream seriousness.
- Jacques Vallée (godfather of ufology, computer scientist) concluded the phenomenon is **NOT extraterrestrial** — same beings, different mask.
- "Hitchhiker effect": studying or witnessing UAPs invites paranormal events into the witness's home/family — Lou Elizondo himself reported green orbs after taking the Pentagon job.
- Imran's thesis: Jinn manifestations packaged in technological clothing for a tech-secular age. Lights, orbs, abductions, gray-alien forms, telepathic messages — all match jinn capabilities.
- The abduction phenomenon has Islamic precedent: a man during Caliph Umar's time disappeared for years after being abducted by jinn, fought over by good jinn, then returned.

## On Pirs / "Spiritual Healers" / Dargahs
- Most are frauds. A few do genuinely engage jinn — but to control a jinn requires self-degradation and acts that go against pure spirituality.
- Anyone claiming to control a "good" jinn is suspect — good jinn don't engage humans at all, so any contact is likely a deceiving evil one in disguise.
- Islamic stance: don't engage; develop your direct connection to God instead. Use *Ayat al-Kursi* (the Throne Verse) as protection — interestingly, the Prophet ﷺ learned this protection FROM Shaytan himself, who told the truth on this one occasion.

## Practical Notes
- Intuition / "bad vibes" in certain places = the spiritual sense (the "sixth sense" of the heart/soul).
- Positive thoughts can also be inspiration from a higher source; true dreams still occur (revelation ended with the Prophet ﷺ).
- Jinn naturally inhabit desolate places — deserts, ruins, dirty/uninhabited areas — pushed there after the angelic war.
